Understanding Depression

Depression is more than temporary sadness. It can affect emotions, thoughts, sleep, work, relationships and daily life. Professional counselling helps you understand your feelings and build healthy coping strategies.

Common Signs

  • Persistent sadness
  • Loss of interest
  • Low motivation
  • Fatigue
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Changes in sleep
  • Feeling hopeless
