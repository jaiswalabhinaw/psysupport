Why Do I Overthink So Much?

Overthinking happens when the mind repeatedly replays situations, future scenarios, or past conversations.

Why It Happens

Often it is connected to anxiety and the desire for control. The brain tries to protect you by analyzing everything.

How to Calm It

• Notice when it starts • Shift attention to breathing • Write thoughts down • Focus on action instead of analysis
